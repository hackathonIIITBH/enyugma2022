Technical Event

The festival offers aspiring innovators a once-in-a-lifetime chance to demonstrate their technological expertise and serves as the ideal platform for innovative ideas. These Events are Planned for the tech-savvy youth by entrepreneurs and tech gurus. A series of challenges, hackathons, and workshops to encourage techies to think creatively and broaden their perspectives These events aim to inspire and empower the Technoids to invent and create new things, as well as to expose them to a wide range of contemporary technology and allow them to interact with other technophiles from all over the nation. Take part in whatever strikes your fancy and listen to what the fest has to say!

Cultural Event

Exhibit your creativity and love for dance, singing, music, debate, poetry, photography, animation, video editing, art and many more . A treasure hunt will be held for everyone to channel their inner Sherlock Holmes in addition to the competitions. You can improve your artistic and expressive abilities by attending workshops.
