Bit-by-Bit

Bit-by-Bit is a hackathon organized in Enyugma (Techno Cultural fest of IIIT Bhagalpur). In continuation, This year Bit-by-Bit is organizing to gather new innovations, new ideas and new skills.
Hackathon is organized in two phase:
First Phase : Idea Submission
In this phase, Teams will show their ideas to the judging team. After evaluating the ideas, we will select teams to participate in the next phase.
After this phase, 15 teams will be selected for next phase.
Second Phase : Project Submission ( 36 hours hackathon )
In this phase, Team has to develop prototype according to their Idea. This round will be conducted in offline mode at our college (IIIT Bhagalpur).

Brand War

Round 1 :-
The team will be provided the brands or company name by us. 24 hour will be given to the participants for preparing the presentation of the brand.They have to submit their strategy and presentation in ppt format in the google form. The team will also have to answer some questions.
Top 10 - 15 (may be extended, depending upon the no. of registrations) teams will qualify for this round and will be eligible for Round 2
Round 2 :-
Now the selected team for round 2 has given a presentation explaining about their work like marketing strategy, sales strategy, used by the company, etc.Depending upon the judgment, the final result will be declared and the top 3 teams will get prizes.

Codeloop

Codeloop is a knockout competition organized on the theme of the traversal of a binary tree. Participants will take part in a knockout tournament where in every round two participants will be matched up against another participant. The winner will advance to the next round and the other participant will be eliminated from the competition. The last person standing will be declared the winner of the competition

FallStack

Fall-Stack is the flagship coding competition of IIIT Bhagalpur organised during ENYUGMA during fall season of every year. This is a two hour coding contest with questions of difficulties such that it is suitable for participants ranging from beginners to Expert. The Contest is guaranteed to Introduce you to New learnings and novel Approaches.

Find the Gem

In this competition, the teams need to make an automated locomotive bot that can navigate througha maze and collect the maximum number of gems they find on the route using computer vision in minimum time.

Robot Specifications:

  1. The bot size should not exceed 15cm*15cm*10cm.
  2. Bot should not consist of any sensors. (Eg:- Camera, IR Sensors, Distance Sensors, etc.)
  3. Bots can only include microcontrollers, motor drivers, motors, chassis, wheels.

Oracle of Delphi

Oracle of Delphi is an AI/ML hackathon which is a part of Enyugma, an annual Techno-Cultural fest organized by IIIT Bhagalpur where developers all over India compete trying to get highest accuracy on provided dataset. All intellectual property of all the source code of the model submitted during this competition will belong to IIIT Bhagalpur which can be used in research in the field.
