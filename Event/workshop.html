Workshop-cybersecurity

Three Workshops which will cover How a hacker uses social Engineering, Information available openly on internet to gain unauthorized access and how he hacks devices such as Routers, LANs, Hotspots, etc.

Human Deception

  • Workshop on awareness of the real world attacks and frauds.
  • Live demonstration of the methods used by attackers.

Wireless Hacking

  • Demonstration of several types of wireless hacking including Mobile/ Laptop hotspots, Routers,LAN, etc.
  • Awaring the audience about their password strengths and securing them.
  • Will Familiarise the audience with tools commonly used in Wireless hacking.

Open Source Intelligence

  • Demonstrating how the data available openly in the Internet can lead to gathering information, which could lead to disasters.
  • This workshop will describe the efficient use of raw data available to everyone.
  • It will primarily focus on the general techniques and tools used in the modern day world.
